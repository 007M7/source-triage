# Optional Feedback

This Skill does not collect telemetry, upload sources, or automatically store feedback.

If you choose to share feedback with a maintainer, keep it short and remove private or copyrighted source content. A useful voluntary note can include:

```text
source type: article | PDF | report | book | other
triage decision used:
what you did next: skipped | quick read | deep read | deferred
was the decision useful: yes | partly | no
what was missing or misleading:
```

Feedback is anecdotal input, not an automated metric or causal proof that the Skill works. Do not include credentials, personal data, customer data, full source text, or unpublished material.
